Melbourne, Australia, November 2017

When

Starts on the afternoon of Friday November 10th, and runs until midday on Monday 13th.

Where

At Camp Toolangi, in the Yarra Valley northeast of Melbourne.

Can Haz?

Please do come along! If you'd like to register, keep an eye on the announcement site - tickets go on sale August 1 at 9am AEST.

Please Note

Please be aware that we will not be providing alcoholic beverages at Rails Camp Melbourne as part of your ticket purchase.
However, you are welcome to BYO into the camp.
As always, we ask that you drink responsibly, and be respectful of those that do not wish to imbibe
