Brisbane, Australia, May 2018

When

Starts on the afternoon of Friday May 18th, and runs until 10am on Monday 21st.

Where

At YMCA's Camp North Pine, next to Old Petrie Town in the North of Brisbane.

Can Haz?

Please do come along! If you'd like to register, tickets are now on sale. A very limited number of camping tickets will also be available.

Please Note

Please be aware that we will not be providing alcoholic beverages at Rails Camp North Pine as part of your ticket purchase.
However, you are welcome to BYO into the camp.
As always, we ask that you drink responsibly, and be respectful of those that do not wish to imbibe
