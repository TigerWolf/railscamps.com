Adelaide, Australia, November 2008

When

Starts on the afternoon of Friday November 14th, and runs until midday on Monday 17th.

Where

At the Woodhouse Activity Centre, close to Adelaide in South Australia.

Can Haz?

Please do come along! If you'd like to register, you can either book a bed or a tent spot, which will cost you $100 or $80, respectively. But places are limited in both cases, so don't wait too long to reserve a spot!
